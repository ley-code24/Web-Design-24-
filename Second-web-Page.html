His fluffy black and white fur made him stand out among all the other animals. Every day, Sam would wake up to the sound of birds chirping and the rustling of bamboo leaves. He loved nothing more than to spend his days munching on juicy bamboo shoots and rolling around in the soft grass. He spotted a rabbit in danger. He rescued a trapped rabbit, became the protector of the forest, and made friends with other animals. Sam surprised his friends with a magical evening among fireflies, teaching them that kindness and a touch of magic can make dreams come true. A giant baby panda known for his bravery and kindness became a true warrior.

Get to Know The Panda Facts:

Panda's like Sam is Know for...

Pandas are known for their distinctive black and white fur, bamboo-based diet, gentle nature, endangered status, and symbolic significance in wildlife conservation efforts. They are recognized for their iconic appearance and serve as ambassadors for the conservation of endangered species and their habitats worldwide.

Breading Facts

  1. A female can mate with several males, who compete over her.
  2. Pandas normally give birth to single young
  3. The reproductive rate is about 1 cub every 2 years.

Adult facts

  • Each adult has a well-defined home range.
  • While roaming their territories, they mark their routes by spraying urine, clawing tree trunks, and rubbing against objects.
  • But they can live up to 30 years in captivity.
